BEHIND THE BUILD.

I'm a Mendix developer, software architect, frontend developer and UI designer based in Belgium. Five years in — building enterprise platforms that thousands of people use every day, architecting systems that scale, and designing interfaces that feel intuitive.

What sets me apart is the full-stack perspective. I can sit in an architecture review in the morning, give feedback on a Figma prototype at noon, and push production code in the afternoon. Clients don't need to translate between disciplines — I speak all of them.
